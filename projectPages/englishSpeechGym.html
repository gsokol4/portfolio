I remember watching my grandma making home baked chocolate chip cookies. Her work demonstrated something to me. Good work takes dedication and I like cookies.

My goal with this website was to build my project from the ground up, to take a project from 0 to 100 without using frameworks or a CMS. While these tools are great (and a time saver), I wanted to go another route to show flexibility. Taking the do-it-yourself route allows me to demonstrate the depth and width of my skills. Not only coding but my ability to design and my abilities to manage and create media. I am the architect of the website and it’s file structures.The design of each page is unique and something imagined by me. Finally, the styles are all coded from scratch just like grandma used to bake’em.
